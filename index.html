About Us

Most. Value. Possible.

The goal at MVP Printing is to make 3D Printing faster and more accessible for anyone. This is why we use fast, quality printers like the Bambu Labs X1 Carbon and the Creality K1 Max, so quality parts can be printed and delivered easily and a lower cost.

learn more

Student Run Business

MVP Printing is a small printing service owned and operated by Matthew Franke, a college student who loves 3D Printing.

This service was created for two reasons:

1. 3D Printers are expensive, and sometimes you only want to print one thing.

2. College is expensive, and sometimes you need a side hustle to help you get by.

Ordering through MVP Prints not only makes it cheaper to print that helmet you've always wanted, but it also helps put someone through school!

Matthew Franke
